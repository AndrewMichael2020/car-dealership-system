About Us

Welcome to Pacific Auto Group, your trusted partner in finding high-quality vehicles at competitive prices. We offer a wide selection of domestic and imported cars, ensuring reliability and affordability for every customer.

Liam Chen

CEO & Founder

Liam Chen has built Pacific Auto Group on a foundation of integrity and innovation. With over two decades in the automotive industry, he has developed a reputation for prioritizing customer needs and ensuring an exceptional car-buying experience. His extensive knowledge of the Canadian vehicle market allows him to source reliable and competitively priced cars. Under his leadership, Pacific Auto Group has expanded its reach across Vancouver, offering a curated selection of vehicles suited for various budgets and needs.

Sophie Patel

Sales Director

Sophie Patel brings over 15 years of experience in automotive sales, specializing in customer relations and market analysis. She understands that buying a car is a significant decision, and she ensures that every customer is well-informed about their options. Her ability to match clients with vehicles that best suit their lifestyle and financial goals has made her an essential part of Pacific Auto Group. Sophie stays updated on market trends, financing options, and new automotive technologies to provide customers with the best deals possible.

Ethan MacDonald

Head of Customer Support

Ethan MacDonald leads the customer support team with a focus on efficiency and transparency. With a background in automotive service management, he ensures that clients receive clear, prompt, and honest communication. From post-sale service to addressing inquiries about warranties and vehicle history, Ethan prioritizes customer satisfaction. His expertise in handling service requests, resolving concerns, and maintaining long-term client relationships has made Pacific Auto Group a trusted name in the Vancouver automotive industry.
